Social and Dating Coaching

Contact Us Schedule an Appointment
Contact Us Schedule an Appointment
We'll equip you with skills and techniques to feel more at-ease in social situations and dating. We work on managing anxiety, the sound of your voice, body language, humor, and thinking on the spot. We love to help students find the confident and pleasant person within themselves.

We often think of public speaking in terms of delivering a corporate presentation or delivering an address to a large audience. This isn't always the case! You know that in your personal life you speak regularly and often. From dating to impromptu conversations to simply feeling disconnected from your authentic voice, feeling at-ease speaking in social situations is imperative.

You can build and grow the skills and techniques needed to be a confident speaker. There is no need to be nervous, anxious, or panicked when speaking in personal situations. You can learn these competencies and feel confident with the sound of your voice, your ability to speak without preparation, and express yourself in any situation.

Our speech coaches can work with you in a one-to-one setting or in groups to prepare you for any social circumstance.
